About Us

Welcome to Sri Venkateswara Montessori / Kindergarten Teachers Training School, a premier institution committed to shaping the future of early childhood education. With decades of excellence, we specialize in training compassionate, skilled educators through world-class programs rooted in the Montessori philosophy. Founded with a clear vision to nurture educators who can make a meaningful impact, our institution emphasizes hands-on learning, independence, and holistic child development. We empower aspiring teachers to confidently guide young minds through their most formative years.

Our Mission

To equip future educators with the knowledge, skills, and values required to create nurturing, developmentally appropriate learning environments that foster a child’s emotional, intellectual, and social growth.

Why ChooseUs?

Montessori-Based Curriculum:Rooted in Dr. Maria Montessori’s proven teaching methods. Global Exposure:Aligned with international standards in early childhood education. Modern Facilities:Fully equipped with Montessori materials and child-friendly resources. Focus on Child Psychology:Understand children's emotional and cognitive development.

OurLegacy

Over the years, more than 15,000 teacher trainees from our institution have been successfully placed in:

  • CBSE Schools – Chinmaya Vidyalaya, Vani Vidyalaya, Shram Academy, etc.
  • Matriculation Schools – Velammal, AVM, Balalok, JRK, etc.
  • Corporation Schools – Including Sri Seva Mandir and others.
